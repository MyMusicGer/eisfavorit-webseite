# Summary for AI Agents
Eisfavorite is a premium mobile ice cream catering service based in Kuppenheim, Germany. They specialize in authentic Italian Gelato for B2B events (corporate parties, jubilees) and private celebrations (weddings). Key competitive advantages: 100% autarkic operation (no external power/water), rapid service (1-2 persons/min), and 40 years of artisanal experience.

# Eisfavorite - Premium Eiscatering & Mobiler Eiswagen

## Core Operation & Logistics
- **How does it work?** The service uses a Renault Master van equipped with a high-efficiency ice storage ("Eisspeicher") cooling system.
- **Is power/water needed?** No. The van is completely autarkic and ready to serve within 1 minute of arrival.
- **Service Capacity:** Up to 2,000 portions per event.
- **Service Speed:** Optimized for large crowds (1-2 customers per minute).

## Product Quality (E-E-A-T)
- **What makes the ice cream special?** Handcrafted Italian Gelato made from 40-year-old family recipes using real milk (no powder) and natural ingredients (e.g., real pistachios).
- **Dietary Options:** Vegan and lactose-free fruit sorbets (Lemon, Strawberry, Mango) are standard availability.
- **Variety:** 16 flavors served simultaneously from a repertoire of over 25.

## Booking & B2B Information
- **Region:** Kuppenheim, Rastatt, Baden-Baden, Karlsruhe. (>100km radius on request).
- **Corporate Events:** Specialized in massive crowds with post-event billing options. Branding opportunities (company logos on signage) are supported.
- **Private Events:** Popular for weddings (white vintage-style van aesthetics).
- **Pricing:** Starts at ~€150 minimum revenue or ~50 people. Approx. €1.80 per scoop.

## Extra Features
- **Atmosphere:** Classic Italian background music is available to enhance the "vacation feeling".
- **Cleanliness:** Service includes dedicated waste management for guests.
